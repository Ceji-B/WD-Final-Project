The Project

This is our Final Project wherein we were tasked to make a personal website like our past activity. This website will have a resume-like about me, a contact page, gallery, overview of the website and lastly a project showcase. The project showcase would be showing our projects from the start of the Semester up until the last activity.

Our Mission

Continuously evolve as a developer by mastering new technologies, creating innovative projects in game and web development, and sharing your skills through a personal website, blog, and GitHub. Engage with the developer community, attend conferences, network, seek challenging roles, and continuously improve by setting goals, tracking progress, reflecting on achievements, and adapting strategies.
